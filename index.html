FAQ

Good questions, straight answers.

Something else on your mind? Give us a call — a person answers.

How fast can you get here? +

Most jobs get scheduled same-day or next-day. Call before noon and there's a good chance your junk is gone by dinner.

How does pricing work? +

By volume — how much of the truck your items fill. The price we confirm on arrival covers labor, hauling, and disposal. It never changes after we start.

What won't you take? +

Hazardous materials: wet paint, chemicals, fuel, asbestos, and the like. Nearly everything else — furniture, appliances, electronics, debris — is fair game.

Do I need to be home? +

Not necessarily. If the items are accessible — curb, driveway, open garage — we can confirm the price by text and handle it while you're at work.

What happens to my stuff? +

Donation and recycling come first — local charities, schools, and scrap partners. The landfill is our last resort, not our first stop.
